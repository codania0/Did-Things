FAQ
What is Did Things?
Did Things helps you visualize what you’ve already accomplished during the day instead of focusing on unfinished tasks.

Do I need an account?
Yes. An account is required to use the app.
This allows your progress to be securely saved and synced.

Why does Did Things require login?
Login ensures that your data is securely stored and accessible across sessions.
We use secure authentication services to protect your information.

Where is my data stored?
Did Things uses secure cloud services provided by Firebase to store and synchronize your data.

Is my data shared?
No. Your personal data is not sold or shared with third parties.

How can I delete my account?
To request account deletion, email us at 4nia.pro@gmail.com from your registered email address.
